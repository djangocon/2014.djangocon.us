Travel

Traveling to Portland, Or

International Connections

Portland is directly accessible from Schipol in the Netherlands and Tokyo in Japan. There are also direct flights from Hawaii and Anchorage, Alaska. Once in the USA there are direct flights from many domestic airports.

Arriving from the USA

From West coast destinations you can travel to Portland via Amtrak. Most delegates will fly in to Portland International Airport (PDX). From there you can either take a cab (Approximately $45) or the Max light railway ($2.50). This latter form of transport is therefore most economical, and will drop you less than a quarter of a mile from the conference hotel.
