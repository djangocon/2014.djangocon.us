Jacinda Shelly

Jacinda has been a life-long perfectionist with deadlines who knew Django was meant for her when she saw that tagline. She is currently the Lead Platform Engineer at Doctor On Demand, a San Francisco-based startup that allows you to have a video call with a licensed physician from your own home. She loves working on projects that she believes will make a difference in the world.

When not standing (go standing desks!) behind a computer Jacinda can be found rowing, reading, traveling or hanging out with amazing husband.
